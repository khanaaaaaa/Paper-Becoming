Principles

  1. Find a quiet, comfortable position and close your eyes.
  2. Breathe naturally and observe without judgement.
  3. When the mind wanders, ently return to your breath.
  4. Let thoughts pass like clouds - do not hold them.
  5. Sit with stillness for the full duration of your session.
  6. End each session with a moment of gratitude.
